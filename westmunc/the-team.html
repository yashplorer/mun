Rick Da

rdasyberia@gmail.com

Rick Da is a current senior and a Model UN Co-President at San Mateo High School. He joined MUN his sophomore year completely clueless about the system. However, he eventually attended a total of four conferences at Stanford and Berkeley by far and hopes to help the his peers avoid the confusion and discomfort of the first conference. Working with other leaders in SMHS MUN to prepare his fellow club members for the conferences, he has also worked on a comprehensive guidebook for his school's club's reference. While Rick hopes to attract more students to the relatively young club at San Mateo, he also hopes to prepare students for the rigors of the conferences through early exposure, precisely one of WESTMUNC's objectives.

Masao Dahlgren

masao.dahlgren@gmail.com

Now a high school senior, Masao Dahlgren has been involved with Model UN for five years, having had his start in debate during middle school. Having attended countless conferences, both national and international, in both the capacity of a chair and as a delegate, his passion for organized debate is second to none. With such experiences, not to mention his position as both a school-independent delegate and a WestMUNC founding partner, Masao brings a unique perspective to the WestMUNC team which he hopes will translate to a engaging and powerful conference for all attendees. When otherwise unoccupied, Masao takes pleasure in  reading, tinkering, and riding his bicycle.
