WESTMUNC 2014 Registration

Welcome to online registration for WESTMUNC 2014! As the San Mateo Union High School District's premier independent Model UN conference, we hope to provide an informative, challenging and most importantly fun experience for the newest delegates and seasoned veterans alike.
* Required

    Logistical Details

    The conference will be taking place from 9am to 5pm on September 20th at Mills High School on 400 Murchison Dr Millbrae, CA 94030.

    The venue will be administrated by Mills Model UN, and staff from San Mateo HS, Burlingame HS, Mills HS, and Aragon HS will be running the conference proceedings.
    The conference will have three separate committees, each with their unique topics and chairs which delegates will be placed into.

    There will be a morning and afternoon committee session, separated by a lunch break. Delegates are expected to bring their own lunch.

    If necessary, coordinator Masao Dahlgren may be contacted at masao.dahlgren@gmail.com, or by phone at 650-863-3698.
